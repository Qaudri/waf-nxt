Gameplay Format

WAF NXT is a unique hybrid of Tackle Football and Flag Football in a 7-on-7 format. It features intensity and physicality of tackle football x the speed and high scoring action of flag football.

Games will include four (4) twelve-minute (12 minute) quarters with sudden death overtime. There is no kicking game except for punting and onside kicks which teams can only elect to do so in the 4th quarter. Offenses will have four (4) downs to gain ten (10) yards, and a first down will extend drives. Six (6) points are provided for every touchdown scored, and teams can attempt a one (1) point conversion after a touchdown from the five-yard line or a 2pt. conversion from the ten-yard line.

The game will feature a 10-yard endzone and a 100-yard field.

The 7-on-7 format will feature offenses that include a Quarterback, Running Back, Center, two Wide Receivers, and two Tight Ends.

Defenses will feature a Middle Linebacker, two Safeties, two Defensive Ends, and two Cornerbacks.
