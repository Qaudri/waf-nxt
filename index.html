About

Women of American Football (WAF) introduces America's No.1 sport of football into the women's sports arena within the game's first female professional and global ecosystem, powered by Artificial Intelligence (AI). Sports' ground-breaking AI platform will create a smarter, faster, more transparent, and more engaging product for teams, athletes and most importantly, fans.

WAF NXT will debut a youth-to-professional platform to develop girls through dedicated camps and a global pathway program, while providing adult women the opportunity to compete on the game’s biggest stage for women.

Artificial Intelligence Native

  • On-Field Performance

    • Player development: AI will analyze film, biometrics, and practice data to personalize training, accelerate skill acquisition, and extend athlete careers.
    • Injury prevention: Predictive models flag fatigue and injury risk before breakdowns occur.
    • Game strategy: Real-time and post-game AI analysis identifies tendencies, optimal play calls, and matchup advantages.

  • Officiating & Game Integrity

    • Computer vision: Automated ball tracking, line-to-gain accuracy, and objective replay review.
    • Consistency: Reduced human error without slowing the game.

  • Fan Experience

    • Personalized viewing: AI-driven broadcasts tailored by team, player, or storyline.
    • Second-screen intelligence: Live predictions, win probabilities, and play explanations in real time.
    • Immersive access: AI-enhanced camera selection, mic'd-up insights, and interactive replays.

  • Media, Content & Storytelling

    • Automated highlights: Instant, shareable content built for digital and social platforms.
    • Narrative engines: AI surfaces storylines fans care about—rivalries, breakout players, and momentum shifts.
    • Global scale: Localized content creation for international audiences at speed.

  • League & Team Operations

    • Scheduling and logistics: AI optimizes travel, rest, and competitive balance.
    • Roster construction: Data-driven evaluation of talent, contracts, and long-term value.
    • Business intelligence: Smarter pricing, sponsorship valuation, and revenue forecasting.

  • Betting, Prediction & Engagement

    • Predictive modeling: Live probabilities and outcome simulations.
    • Fan participation: Interactive, data-driven engagement without relying on chance-based mechanics.
